### Tags to AVOID (will attract the wrong audience):
- Card Game / Deckbuilding / Card Battler — items use a card UI but NOT a deckbuilder
- JRPG / Anime — combat has Persona-like trait matching but aesthetic is opposite
- Pixel Graphics / Retro — art is realistic 2D, not pixel art
- Dungeon Crawler — not applicable
- Point & Click — has some inspiration but would mislead
- Survival Horror — this is strategic horror, not resource-scarcity survival
- Simulation / Management — despite facility setting, not a management sim
- Roguelite — DLP is closer to roguelike (knowledge persists, not power)

### Tags to investigate (may or may not exist on Steam):
- Analog Horror
- Board Game
- Procedural Generation
- Investigation

## Primary Genre Recommendation

**RPG** — Matches World of Horror, Darkest Dungeon, and Persona. Captures the
stats/dice/equipment/role-playing elements and puts DLP in the same browse category
as its closest comps. Strategy is secondary genre signal via tags.
